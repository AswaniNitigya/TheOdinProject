Here's a simple step-by-step recipe to make Dhokla:

Ingredients

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • ½ cup water
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ginger-green chili paste
  • 1 tsp eno (fruit salt) or baking soda
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • ½ cup curd (optional, for softness)

Steps to Make Dhokla:

  1. Make batter - Mix besan, turmeric, sugar, salt, ginger-chili paste, curd, and water to a smooth consistency.
  2. Add eno - Just before steaming, add eno and lemon juice. Mix well.
  3. Steam - Pour into a greased tray and steam for 15-20 minutes. Check with a toothpick.
  4. Prepare tempering - Heat oil, add mustard seeds, green chilies, curry leaves, and sesame seeds. Pour in sugar water.
  5. Pour over dhokla - Let it soak for a few minutes.
  6. Cut & serve - Garnish with coriander and grated coconut (optional).
