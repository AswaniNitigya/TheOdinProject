Here's a simple step-by-step recipe to make South Indian-style filter coffee:

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp filter coffee powder
  • 1 cup hot water
  • ½ to ¾ cup milk (as per preference)
  • 1 to 2 tsp sugar (adjust to taste)

Steps to Make Filter Coffee:

  1. Add coffee powder - Put 2 tbsp of filter coffee powder into the upper chamber of the coffee filter.
  2. Pour hot water - Boil water and pour about 1 cup over the coffee powder. Close the lid and let it drip slowly into the lower chamber (takes 10–15 minutes).
  3. Prepare milk - Heat milk without boiling. Adjust quantity based on how strong you want the coffee.
  4. Mix with decoction - Take about ¼ to ⅓ cup of the brewed decoction and mix with hot milk.
  5. Add sugar - Stir in sugar as per taste.
  6. Froth the coffee - Pour the coffee back and forth between a tumbler and davara (or two cups) to create froth.
  7. Serve hot - Enjoy your strong, aromatic filter coffee!
